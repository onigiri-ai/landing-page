For inquiring minds

What is "Onigiri.ai"?

Onigiri is an intelligent investor recommendation engine, investor introduction network and a fundraising coaching service.

Will I be connected with investors?

Yes! We do require additional vetting for this service to ensure we are referring legitimate startups. We will not share your information to investors without your prior consent.

I've never fundraised. Will Onigiri help me?

We designed Onigiri for founders like yourself!

Onigiri is built with input from both first-time entrepreneurs and experienced founders, and is currently designed to serve the needs of early-stage fundraising.

How much does Onigiri cost?

The Onigiri platform is free of charge to use. We want to help founders succeed!

During the beta, 1-on-1 fundraising coaching sessions are offered pro-bono.

What if I'm currently pre-product and pre-revenue?

Onigiri is most valuable to founders soon to be raising (within the next 3 months), or have recently begun raising external capital (within the past month).

Who has access to my data?

Your data is yours, and it is stored securely, encrypted at rest.

If you request an investor intro, only after you've given us permission to connect you with an investor, will limited information be disclosed to them.
