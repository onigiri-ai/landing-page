For inquiring minds

What is "Onigiri.ai"?

Onigiri is a fundraising management tool for startup founders to streamline their early-stage fundraising operations.

We do this by encoding a workflow that serves as a best practice guide for less experienced founders.

How much does Onigiri cost?

At the moment, Onigiri is free of charge.

Founders that sign up now will have access to the existing features for free forever.

I've never fundraised. Will Onigiri help me?

We designed Onigiri for founders like yourself!

Onigiri is built with input from both first-time entrepreneurs and experienced founders, and is currently designed to serve the needs of early-stage fundraising.

What if I'm currently pre-product and pre-revenue?

Onigiri is most valuable to founders soon to be raising (within the next 6 months), or have recently begun raising external capital (within the past month).

Will I be connected with investors?

Not for the beta, and never without your prior consent.

I have already raised a round. Do I need Onigiri?

Onigiri is designed to serve the needs of founders from Pre-Seed through Series A fundraising. If that's you, Onigiri is the right tool for you.

Who has access to my data?

Only you and those you invite to your team. All data is encrypted at rest with AWS RDS.
